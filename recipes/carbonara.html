Carbonara Recipe

Description

Carbonara is a simple pasta dish that only takes a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Parmessan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Cook bacon
  3. Whisk cheese, pepper, and eggs in a bowl
  4. Add mixture to pasta
  5. Add bacon
  6. Eat
